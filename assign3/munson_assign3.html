My Butt Blew Up. Down to 8 Lives

One night I came inside covered in blood. Mama stayed up all night with me until I could go to the vet in the morning. I had gotten bit in the butt a few days earlier and the abscess exploded. There was barely enough skin left to sew me back together....but they did. For a while I had to wear a cone on my head...it sucked.

I Swallowed a String. Down to 7 Lives

Mama was sewing so I started playing with the thread. When she wasn't looking I tried to eat it. It got wrapped around my tongue and embedded in my intestine. After two surgeries they weren't sure I'd make it. Mama made a schedule so she would remember when to give me I.V. fluids and feed me through my feeding tube. This is me sitting above the schedule...dreaming of the day I got the tube out of my neck and could scratch my ears again.

I Ate a Plant. Down to 6 Lives

Over the holidays I was staying at my grandma's house. I LOVE to chew on her plants. I swallowed a piece of one by mistake and it got stuck inside of me. Mama took me to the hospital on New Years Eve and stayed with me all night long. We were so tired. I had surgery again and now can only eat canned food. I miss my crunchy treats but am glad to be alive. While mama was waiting for me to have surgery, she made this photo collage. Tell me not to do something and I'll do it twice...and take pictures! What can I say, I take after my mama!
